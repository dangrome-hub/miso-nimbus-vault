⚠️ Things to Watch Out For
🚪 Front Door Runners: Both cats have a strong habit of lurking right near the front door. Try not to leave it open or ajar for any extended period, or they will make a break for it down the hall!
👣 Foot Hazards: They love weaving directly around your feet and shins while you walk. Keep a close eye out on the floor—especially when navigating the stairs or busy inside the kitchen.
🥣 Feeding Schedule & Prep

They eat three times a day. Please try to space their meals roughly 7 hours apart.

  • Portion Size: Each cat gets exactly one pouch (65g) per meal. If they seem noticeably ravenous across the day, you can offer them one extra booster pouch to share between them.
  • Food Prep Hack: To warm the food up properly, pop the sealed pouches straight into a metal bowl, pour boiling water over them, and let them sit for 90 seconds before cutting them open.
  • Equipment: Clean plates are stacked and ready in the drawer situated right next to the fridge.
🧶 Play & Cuddles
  • Energy Levels: They are absolute high-energy maniacs first thing in the morning and late at night, but they predictably crash and snooze heavily from around 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
  • Toys: All of their toys live inside the media cabinet stored underneath the main TV.
  • Boundaries: When they are completely hyper, give them their own space. When they are sleepy, they love deep cuddles. If they ever nip at you, simply redirect their attention by tossing a toy.

The master white buttons mounted by the front door and directly at the bedside control all light channels. Hold any active switch down to dim, and release when you like the level. For the automated blinds, a single clean tap of the Up or Down button is all it takes.

📺 Music & Television:
Download the WiiM app onto your phone to easily stream music throughout the entire house. For the main TV, pick up the white remote to manage all streaming apps. If the audio output ever starts crackling, grab the black remote and simply cycle the power button on the receiver.

💩 Litter Box:
One clean scoop per day is plenty. Clean replacement bags are stored in the second island drawer on the left side. Tie the filled waste bags up tightly and toss them directly into the large black wheelie bin located outside.

🛑 One small but important thing: please close doors carefully. The cats love darting through gaps and are very easy to accidentally catch. Mind your feet too!
