🏠 Apartment 34a

Cecil & Chutney Care Instructions

🥣 Feeding Schedule

They are fed twice a day: once in the morning and once in the evening.

  • Wet Food: Take one pouch of wet food and split it evenly between their two primary bowls.
  • Dry Food: Shake a little bit of dry kibble into their shared middle bowl—just enough to lightly cover the bottom surface of the dish.
💩 Litter Box Maintenance
  • Location: Their main litter box is located inside the downstairs bathroom.
  • Routine: Please scoop the box once a day. You can drop the waste directly into the toilet and flush it away instantly. No bags needed!
☀️ Summer Weather Protocols

We worry about the cats overheating in the summer. Please keep an eye on the forecast:

🌡️ IF IT EXCEEDS +28°C DURING THE DAY:
  • 🌅 In the Morning: Shut all windows completely tight and pull the window blinds mostly down to lock out the baking daytime sun.
  • 🌇 In the Evening: Open the windows up and raise the blinds to allow cooler night air to circulate through the flat.
🌅 Terrace & Balcony Rules (For Myrtle)

Myrtle, you are more than welcome to hang out up on the terrace and enjoy the outdoor space! If you do, the cats are very welcome to join you outside, but please follow these strict safety boundaries:

🔒 When you are not actively up there: The balcony window must remain completely closed and locked at all times.
🚪 The Top Room Door: Keep the door leading to the top room closed by default, as that room gets far too hot for the cats. It is totally fine to leave it open while you are out on the terrace with them, but it must be closed when you leave.
🏃‍♂️ Before Leaving the Apartment: You must herd both cats back downstairs into the main flat, ensure they are inside, and securely shut both the balcony window and the top room door.

😏 The Secret Recall Trick: If Cecil or Chutney are playing hard to get and won't come inside, give their bag of food or treat box a loud, energetic shake. They will come running inside instantly!
